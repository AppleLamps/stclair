ANDREW L. CARTER, JR., United States District Judge:

As discussed at today’s telephonic conference, the Court hereby **ORDERS** the parties to submit a joint status report by April 2, 2026. In that status report, the parties should address whether the case has been settled. If not, the parties should inform the Court of whether they would like a referral to the Court’s annexed mediation program or if they’d like the Court to preside over a settlement conference or refer to a magistrate judge for one. If not, the parties can propose a joint briefing schedule on Defendants’ proposed motion to dismiss. Further, Defendants’ deadline to file an answer to the amended complaint is hereby **adjourned sine dine**.

SO ORDERED.
